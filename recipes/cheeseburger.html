Cheeseburger

Cheeseburger with all the fixings

Cooked to perfection cheeseburger. Comes with traditional toppings and condiments

Ingredients

  • 2 gloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 4 slices pepperjack cheese
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 2 oz pickle slices
  • 4 tomato slices

Recipe Instructions

  1. Preheat grill for high heat
  2. Mix garlic, onion, and ground beef in a medium bowl. Form into four patties.
  3. Brush grill grate with oil. Grill hamburger patties 5 minutes per side, or until well done. Top with pepperjack cheese, pickles, tomatos and ketchup and serve on buns.
